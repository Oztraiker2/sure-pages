Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This application is for personal, non-commercial use only.

This application connects to bank accounts through Enable Banking / Open Banking in order to display account balances and transaction history inside Sure.

The application only accesses data that the user explicitly authorizes through their bank. Banking login credentials are not stored by this application.

The data accessed may include account information, balances, and transaction history, strictly for personal financial visibility and account monitoring.

No personal banking data is sold or shared with third parties.

Access to bank data depends on the consent granted by the user through their bank or through the Enable Banking authorization flow. Consent can be revoked at any time through the bank or the relevant consent management tools.

This application is provided as-is for personal use.
